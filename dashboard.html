Tax Planning Tips

  • Invest in ELSS, PPF, or NPS under Section 80C to save up to ₹1,50,000.
  • Claim House Rent Allowance (HRA) if you pay rent.
  • Avail deductions on home loan interest (₹2,00,000 limit).
  • Use Health Insurance (80D) to get deductions up to ₹25,000.
  • Consider donations (80G) for additional tax benefits.

💰 Investment & Wealth Management Tips

  • Diversify Investments – Reduce risk by spreading funds across assets.
  • Invest Long-Term – Compounding works best over time.
  • Manage Risk – Keep an emergency fund and avoid high-risk bets.
  • Tax Optimization – Use tax-saving investment options.
  • Regular Investing – Use SIPs for consistent investments.
  • Avoid Debt Traps – Limit loans and manage credit wisely.
  • Stay Updated – Monitor market trends and adjust strategy.
  • Seek Expert Advice – Consult financial advisors.
